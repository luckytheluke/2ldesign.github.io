Established in 1996 in Prijedor, Polaris Dental Clinic desired to step into the 21st century with a clean, modern, and simple design that would encompass their every medium of communication. From the Logo to the website, following a clearly defined design language to present Polaris Dental as a serious contender in this new day and age.
